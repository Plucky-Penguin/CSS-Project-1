Who is Howard "Howey" Nock?

Howey Nock was born on April 13, 2005 in Cleveland Ohio.

After nailing his interview, he was invited to join University

School during his Kindergartin year. In his free time, Howey

protects the citizens of the Cleveland area from dastardly

villians who aim to control mankind. It can be a grewling

effort, but its always worth it when he sees his loved ones

get home safe. Howey will never give anyone his Reddit,

unless it is by accident.
